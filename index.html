My Story

From a robotics team in South Central LA to leading AI strategy in Silicon Valley.

My journey into technology began on a FIRST Robotics team in South Central Los Angeles. That experience ignited a passion for building things that matter -- and shaped my belief in the transformative power of STEM education.

Today, I lead efforts to close the opportunity gap in STEM education at the Silicon Valley Education Foundation. With over 17 years as an educator, curriculum designer, and robotics mentor, I build AI tools and systems that help teachers teach better and students learn faster.

I don't just build technology. I build solutions that respect the complexity of the classroom and empower educators to do their best work.
