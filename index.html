Who I am

I’m Jose Quijas Castro. A leader in AI-driven education and program design.

With over 17 years in STEM education and technology, I specialize in developing systems that empower educators and provide personalized learning experiences for students. I currently lead AI initiatives at the Silicon Valley Education Foundation, where I focus on creating scalable, equitable educational tools.

What drives me

I believe in using AI to make education more accessible, efficient, and effective. By building tools that support both teachers and students, I aim to foster a future where every student, regardless of background, has the resources to succeed.
