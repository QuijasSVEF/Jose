What is Jose Quijas Castro's approach to AI in education?

Jose's approach to AI in education is built on three core principles:

  • Human-Centered AI: Technology should serve real human needs, focusing on measurable impact for both teachers and students rather than technology for its own sake
  • Equity and Access: AI tools must be designed to reduce, not amplify, educational inequities, ensuring all students benefit regardless of background or resources
  • Scalable Automation: Building systems that reduce administrative burden on educators while maintaining the human elements that make education effective

This approach ensures that AI enhances rather than replaces the crucial human elements of teaching and learning.
