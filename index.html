About Me

As the Director of Computer Science and AI at the Silicon Valley Education Foundation (SVEF), I lead efforts to close the opportunity gap in STEM education, ensuring that all students—particularly those from underserved communities—have access to the skills and knowledge needed for the tech-driven future.

With over 17 years of experience as an educator, curriculum designer, and robotics mentor, I am passionate about integrating AI and computer science into scalable, hands-on programs that prepare students for leadership in tomorrow's world. My journey began on a FIRST Robotics team in South Central LA, which ignited my love for technology and shaped my belief in the transformative power of STEM education.

Today, I focus on creating innovative curricula that blend cutting-edge AI, coding, and robotics with critical thinking and ethical practices. Through my work at SVEF, I collaborate with school districts, educators, and industry leaders to build programs that empower students to thrive in emerging technologies.
